OneTrust Excel Online Add-In (Beta)

Installation

This is the installation documentation page for the OneTrust Excel Online Add-In.


  • Download the xml file for the Add-In here.

  • Open Microsoft Office on the web.

  • In Get started with the online apps now, choose Excel and then open a new document.

  • Open the Insert tab on the ribbon and, in the Add-ins section, choose Office Add-ins.

  • On the Office Add-ins dialog, select the MY ADD-INS tab, choose Manage My Add-ins, and then Upload My Add-in.

  • Browse to the add-in manifest file, and then select Upload.

  • Verify that your add-in is installed. It should appear on the ribbon.
